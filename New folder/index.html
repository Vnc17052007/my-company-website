About Us

Global export support built on clarity, consistency, and trust.

HBC Exports helps businesses connect with reliable markets, verified suppliers, and export-ready processes. From sourcing to shipment coordination, we focus on disciplined communication and dependable execution.

Company Introduction

We are a Palanpur-based export company serving buyers with practical sourcing, documentation, packaging, and logistics coordination.

Mission

To make international trade smoother for our customers through honest guidance, responsive support, and well-managed export workflows.

Vision

To become a trusted Indian export partner known for quality, transparency, and long-term relationships across global markets.

Why Customers Trust Us

  • Clear communication
  • Verified coordination
  • Timely follow-ups
  • Customer-first service

Services

Six export services designed for dependable trade.

Product Sourcing

Verified supplier coordination for textiles, consumer goods, industrial products, food items, and custom buyer requirements.

Export Documentation

Commercial invoice, packing list, certificate support, compliance coordination, and shipment paperwork assistance.

Logistics Coordination

Freight planning, packaging guidance, shipment tracking, and communication between buyers, suppliers, and carriers.

Private Label Support

Assistance with custom packaging, labeling, inspection schedules, and brand-ready product presentation.

Quality Checks

Pre-dispatch coordination, sample management, quality review workflows, and buyer-specific inspection requirements.

Trade Consulting

Practical guidance for pricing, market entry, supplier comparison, and export process planning.
